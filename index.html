What we believe

Community-led solutions

Communities need support to build their own solutions — not someone to come build or sell a solution for them.

Beyond the standard mould

Entrepreneurs come in all forms. It is too hard for those who don't fit the standard mould, and we want to change that.

Values over adulation

True victory is one dogged with strong values and stories of perseverance — not stories of adulation or discrimination.

Thoughtful capital

We are a community of friends and limited partners that steer time, resources, and capital to companies we want to see win.
